Our Journey

2015

Ingathering Global began as a small prayer group in Nigeria, birthed from a vision to ignite revival across the nations.

2018

The ministry expanded across regions, establishing new cells and outreaches to touch the hearts of communities.

2021

Our online ministry launched, reaching thousands globally through digital evangelism and weekly live streams.

2025

Now a global family of believers — raising disciples, transforming nations, and preparing the world for the harvest.
